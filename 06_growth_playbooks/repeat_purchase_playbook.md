## Core Retention Principle

Premium tea retention is not a generic email cadence. It is a replenishment and trust system by product, segment, and consumption cycle.

## Retention Metrics

| Metric | Review Frequency | Decision Trigger [SYNTHETIC] |
|---|---|---|
| Repeat purchase rate | Monthly by cohort | Below 20% by Day 60 |
| Time to second purchase | Weekly for eligible cohorts | Median rises above product replenishment window by 10+ days |
| Second-order AOV | Monthly | Drops below first-order AOV without mix explanation |
| Retention flow revenue | Weekly | Below 10% of D2C revenue |
| Contribution margin from retention | Weekly | Retention revenue grows but CM% falls |

## Kill, Fix, Scale Rules

| Signal | Decision |
|---|---|
| Flow increases repeat purchase and CM% holds | SCALE |
| Open/click signal exists but purchase is weak | FIX message, timing, or product mapping |
| Flow drives orders only through heavy discounting | FIX or KILL depending on CM impact |
| Gift Buyer flow produces no self-purchase after two cycles | KILL current angle and rebuild around story or occasion |
| Product replenishment reminder fires before realistic consumption window | FIX timing |
