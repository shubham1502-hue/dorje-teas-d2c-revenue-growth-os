# Weekly Revenue Review Template

Use this template to run a concise founder-facing D2C revenue review. Replace every bracketed field before using it for an actual company review.

All sample content in this file is illustrative. Do not treat it as Dorje performance or any other company's actual performance.

## Purpose

Turn weekly revenue, acquisition, retention, funnel, and margin signals into founder decisions.

This memo should answer:

- what changed this week
- whether growth quality improved or weakened
- what needs founder judgment
- what the operator will do next
- what should be reviewed again next week

## Owner

| Role | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Founder | Decide budget, pricing, priority, brand risk, tradeoffs, and timing |
| Founder's Office / Operator | Prepare memo, synthesize signals, recommend decisions, log outcomes |
| Growth | Explain channel and funnel movements |
| CRM / Retention | Explain repeat purchase, subscription, lifecycle, and winback movements |
| Ops / Finance | Explain margin, inventory, fulfillment, and cost movements |

## Inputs Needed

- order export for the review period
- channel and campaign spend
- sessions, add-to-cart, checkout, and purchase data
- product and SKU revenue
- new vs. returning customer split
- repeat purchase or cohort view
- subscription activity, if relevant
- discount, COGS, packaging, shipping, and payment fee assumptions
- experiment tracker
- risk register or blocker list

## Weekly Agenda

1. What changed this week?
2. Did revenue grow from the right source?
3. Are CAC, ROAS, and contribution margin aligned?
4. Where is the funnel leaking?
5. Are first-time buyers coming back?
6. Which experiments need scale, fix, kill, or wait decisions?
7. What founder decision is needed this week?

## Metrics Reviewed

| Area | Metrics | Decision it supports |
|---|---|---|
| Revenue | Net revenue, orders, AOV, new vs. returning revenue | Is revenue quality improving? |
| Acquisition | Spend, CAC, ROAS, contribution ROAS | Which channels should scale, hold, fix, or pause? |
| Funnel | Sessions, add-to-cart, checkout, purchase CVR | Where is the leak? |
| Product | SKU revenue, AOV, discount rate, product CM | Which products deserve more traffic or margin review? |
| Retention | Repeat purchase, time to second order, returning revenue | Are customers coming back profitably? |
| Subscription | Active subscribers, renewals, churn, attach rate | Is subscription improving LTV or creating churn risk? |
| Margin | COGS, packaging, shipping, discounts, payment fees, paid media | Are we scaling profitable revenue? |
