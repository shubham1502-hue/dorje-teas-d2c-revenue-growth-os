## Why Gifting Is a Separate Commercial Track

Gift Buyer behavior is not daily tea behavior. A gift order can have high AOV and weak repeat. It needs separate landing pages, separate margin assumptions, separate lifecycle follow-up, and a separate self-purchase conversion metric.

## Gift Box Operating Rules

1. Track Selim Hill Gift Box Classic and Selim Hill Gift Box Premium separately from daily products.
2. Gross revenue is not enough. Review packaging, shipping, gateway fees, and paid media.
3. Gift Buyer post-purchase flow should not use replenishment language.
4. Gift box landing pages need packaging proof, delivery promise, and story card preview.
5. Gift campaigns should report self-purchase conversion at Day 45.

## Corporate Gifting Operating System

| Step | Owner | Output |
|---|---|---|
| Inquiry capture | Founder or Revenue Ops | Corporate gift inquiry form |
| Qualification | Founder or Revenue Ops | Quantity, delivery date, budget, packaging need |
| Quote | Finance/Ops | Price, shipping, packaging, margin estimate |
| Approval | Founder | Go/no-go based on margin and delivery risk |
| Fulfillment tracking | Ops | Dispatch and delivery status |
| Follow-up | CRM | Buyer self-purchase and next-window reminder |

## Gift Buyer to Self-Purchaser Flow

| Timing | Message | Decision Metric |
|---|---|---|
| Day 3 | Gift shipment and story card confirmation | Delivery confidence |
| Day 14 | Soft self-purchase prompt: try what you gifted | Click-to-purchase rate |
| Day 45 | Modest self-purchase offer or First Flush Darjeeling note | Gift Buyer self-purchase conversion |

## Metrics and Decision Rules

| Metric Pattern | Decision |
|---|---|
| Gift AOV high and CM% positive | Scale seasonal gift traffic carefully |
| Gift AOV high and CM% weak | Fix packaging, shipping, or discount logic |
| Gift Buyer self-purchase above 15% [SYNTHETIC] | Treat gifting as acquisition channel |
| Gift Buyer self-purchase near zero | Treat gifting as seasonal revenue only |
| Delivery complaint rate rises in gift window | Stop scaling and fix fulfillment |
