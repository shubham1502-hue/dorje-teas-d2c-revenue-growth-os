# Revenue Risk Register - Dorje Teas D2C Revenue Growth OS

> Public-information based risk register. No internal Dorje risk or performance data used.

### Risk 1: Paid Acquisition Scales but Contribution Margin Collapses
**Likelihood:** Medium
**Impact if realised:** High
**Early warning signal:** ROAS looks acceptable while contribution ROAS falls below 1.0 [SYNTHETIC].
**Mitigation:** Require CM check before every spend increase.
**Owner:** Founder's Office / Revenue Ops

### Risk 2: First Flush Stockout During Peak Season
**Likelihood:** Medium
**Impact if realised:** High
**Early warning signal:** First Flush Darjeeling sessions and add-to-cart grow while available inventory tightens.
**Mitigation:** Add stock visibility to weekly review and prepare Second Flush Darjeeling handoff.
**Owner:** Ops

### Risk 3: Premium Positioning Eroded by Discount Dependency
**Likelihood:** Medium
**Impact if realised:** High
**Early warning signal:** Discount rate exceeds 15% on full-price products [SYNTHETIC].
**Mitigation:** Use price-per-cup and estate proof before discounting.
**Owner:** Founder / Revenue Ops

### Risk 4: Subscription Churn Spike from Over-Supply
**Likelihood:** Medium
**Impact if realised:** Medium
**Early warning signal:** Tea Club churn rises and cancel survey mentions too much tea.
**Mitigation:** Add pause and monthly-to-quarterly cadence options.
**Owner:** Retention / CRM

### Risk 5: Gift Buyer Cohort Produces No Self-Purchase Conversion
**Likelihood:** High
**Impact if realised:** Medium
**Early warning signal:** Gift Buyer self-purchase below 5% by Day 45 [SYNTHETIC].
**Mitigation:** Build Day 14 and Day 45 gift-to-self flows.
**Owner:** Retention / CRM

### Risk 6: Logistics Failure During Diwali Gifting Window
**Likelihood:** Medium
**Impact if realised:** High
**Early warning signal:** Delivery complaint rate rises as gift order volume increases.
**Mitigation:** Separate gift delivery SLA tracking from normal orders.
**Owner:** Ops

### Risk 7: Darjeeling Loyalist Segment Lost to Stronger Harvest Communication by Competitor
**Likelihood:** Medium
**Impact if realised:** Medium
**Early warning signal:** First Flush Darjeeling CTR and direct traffic soften during harvest window.
**Mitigation:** Publish harvest updates, tasting notes, and estate proof before product availability.
**Owner:** Founder / Content

### Risk 8: Health/Wellness Consumer Segment Poached by Better-Positioned Green Tea Brand
**Likelihood:** Medium
**Impact if realised:** Medium
**Early warning signal:** Darjeeling Green Tea CVR trails traffic growth.
**Mitigation:** Create benefit-led Health/Wellness Consumer landing page with clean sourcing proof.
**Owner:** Growth

### Risk 9: Marketplace Presence Cannibalises Owned D2C Revenue
**Likelihood:** Low
**Impact if realised:** Medium
**Early warning signal:** Owned D2C conversion falls while brand demand appears stable.
**Mitigation:** Track owned D2C separately from marketplace; protect D2C-exclusive education and Tea Club path.
**Owner:** Founder's Office

### Risk 10: Founder/Operator Bandwidth Collapse Breaks Weekly Review Cadence
**Likelihood:** Medium
**Impact if realised:** High
**Early warning signal:** Weekly memo not written before meeting or decisions remain unlogged.
**Mitigation:** Keep Monday review to the top three decisions and archive zombie metrics.
**Owner:** Founder's Office
