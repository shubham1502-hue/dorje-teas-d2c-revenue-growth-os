# Operating Cadence - Dorje Teas D2C Revenue Growth OS

> Public-information based cadence design. No internal Dorje operating process data used.

## Weekly Monday to Friday Rhythm

| Day | Owner | Activity | Output | Time Required |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Monday | Founder's Office | Prepare weekly revenue review before meeting | Weekly memo with top three decisions | 90 minutes |
| Monday | Founder + Operators | Revenue review meeting | Decision log and blockers | 45 minutes |
| Tuesday | Growth | Campaign and landing page actions | Spend changes, page fixes, test setup | 60 minutes |
| Wednesday | CRM | Retention and subscription review | Flow fixes and cohort flags | 60 minutes |
| Thursday | Ops/Finance | Margin and fulfillment review | Cost changes, stock risks, delivery issues | 45 minutes |
| Friday | Founder's Office | Experiment closure and next-week prep | Experiment tracker updates | 45 minutes |

## Weekend Anomaly-Only Monitoring

Weekend monitoring should not become full reporting. Check only:

- Payment or checkout failure.
- Stockout on First Flush Darjeeling, Second Flush Darjeeling, Selim Hill Gift Box Classic, or Selim Hill Gift Box Premium.
- Delivery issue during gifting window.
- Paid spend anomaly or tracking break.

## Weekly Revenue Review Meeting Agenda

1. What changed this week?
2. Is revenue growing from the right source?
3. Are CAC, ROAS, and contribution margin aligned?
4. Which funnel stage is leaking?
5. Is repeat purchase improving?
6. Which experiments need scale, fix, kill, or wait?
7. What decision does the founder need to make today?

## Rules for Decision Logging

- Every decision gets an owner, date, reason, and expected metric movement.
- A decision without a metric is not logged as a decision.
- Founder decisions are separated from operator actions.
- If no action follows the metric, remove the metric from weekly review.

## Rules for Founder Unblocks

- Bring only decisions that require founder judgment: budget, pricing, product priority, brand risk, or tradeoff.
- Do not ask the founder to inspect raw dashboards in the meeting.
- State the recommendation first, then the evidence.
- Include the cost of waiting when timing matters.

## Rules for Experiment Closure

- Every experiment closes as Scale, Fix, Kill, or Wait.
- Wait is allowed only when sample size, timing, or contamination prevents a clean read.
- No experiment runs longer than four weeks without a written reason.
- Learnings must be reusable beyond the tested page or message.
